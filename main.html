Storycraft is a series of alternate future/universe skins in League of Legends. Set in the myth of The Great Beast Galio, champions were characters in the story until the arrival of the Lunar Players, who showed them their fate was no longer set in stone. Stories, myths, legends: within each lies a world unto itself, where characters act out their trials and tribulations over and over as their tales are told and retold.
